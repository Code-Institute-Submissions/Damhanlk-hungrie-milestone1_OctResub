Summer Pesto Pasta

This one is so easy! Add basil, toasted pine nuts, parmesan, olive oil to a food processor. Blitz for 20 seconds.


Meanwhile, boil some of your favourite pasta, we used farfalle


Stir in your fresh pesto. Add cherry tomatoes and serve hot or cold with extra parmesan!
